# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

PhoneAgent contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate professionally,
assume good faith, give technically grounded feedback, and respect differences in
experience and background. Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language,
publication of private information, and deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

## Project-specific expectations

Because PhoneAgent can operate real devices, reports and contributions must not expose
credentials, private conversations, phone numbers, account identifiers, unredacted
trajectories, or other personal data. Security-sensitive issues should follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) rather than being discussed publicly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that violate
these expectations, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Reports
should be sent through the repository's private security reporting channel or directly to
the maintainers through GitHub.

This policy applies in repository spaces and in public spaces when someone is officially
representing the project.
